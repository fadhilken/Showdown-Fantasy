Welcome to Showdown Fantasy League!

Founded by Fadhil Kennedy in 2020, Showdown Fantasy League is an exciting platform for fantasy football enthusiasts to compete and connect with others around the world.

Our league currently has a total of 15 members, including 3 admins who help manage the league and ensure fair play. We welcome anyone who loves football and enjoys the thrill of competition to join us!
