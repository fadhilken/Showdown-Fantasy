Showdown Fantasy League

Showown Fantasy League was created by Fadhil Kennedy in the year 2020 before the commencement of the 20/21 English Premier League season. It is a platform for soccer enthusiasts to showcase their skills in predicting match outcomes, analyzing player performance and competing with others in the league. The league has since grown in popularity and has gained a following worldwide.

Upon the formation of the fantasy league, it also led to an exciting of joining other fantasy leagues including:

  • Uefa Champions League
  • African Cup of Nations(AFCON)
  • The World Cup

The league is open to anyone who is interested in joining and is free of charge. It is a platform where members can interact with each other, share opinions and insights about the game, and form friendships based on their mutual love for soccer. The league is run by a dedicated team of administrators who ensure that the rules are followed and that everything runs smoothly.

The league runs on the official Fantasy Premier League website, which is a platform that allows users to create and manage their own fantasy teams. The league has a set of rules and regulations that govern the game, including transfer deadlines, captaincy rules, and scoring systems. These rules are enforced by the administrators of the league to ensure that everyone plays fairly.

Winners of the league are recognized and celebrated at the end of each season. Trophies and other prizes are given out to the top performers, including recognition on the league's website and social media platforms.
